What Is an Orthodontist?

An orthodontist is a dental specialist who focuses on diagnosing, preventing, and treating issues related to the alignment of teeth and jaws. Unlike general dentists, orthodontists receive additional years of advanced education and clinical training specifically in tooth movement (orthodontics) and facial development.
